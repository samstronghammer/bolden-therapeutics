Who are we?

Who are we? : Bolden Therapeutics is a biotechnology company developing first-in-class therapeutics to treat central nervous system diseases such as Alzheimer’s disease and ischemic stroke. Bolden’s research facility is located in Cambridge, MA, and its corporate headquarters is located in Providence, RI.
